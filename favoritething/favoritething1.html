WHAT IS MUSIC?

Music is the combination of pitch, rhythm, dynamics, and qualities of timbre and texture. Different genres of music compose tracks that vary in the utilization of these fundamentals, and a myriad of means exist for the composition of it.

It is a subject holding a broad number of components with nearly as much depth to them as well, allowing for a means of expression that is unique from other forms of art. It is because of this that music is so fascinating, and it remains a cornerstone of culture worldwide to this day.
